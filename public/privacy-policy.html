Privacy Policy for Tsui Attendance

Effective Date: 16/05/2025

Tsui Tech Solution LLP is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information through our mobile application ("Tsui Attendance") used by sales employees for attendance, location tracking, sales management, and reporting.

1. Information We Collect

  • a. Personal Information
    • Name, phone number, email address (for account creation and login)
    • Photo (uploaded by employee or admin)
    • Salary details (via uploaded Excel files)
  • b. Location Data
    • Background and foreground GPS location data to track attendance and sales routes
    • (Location tracking occurs even when the app is in the background)
  • c. App Usage Data
    • Attendance timestamps
    • Shop and sales data
    • Reports generated within the app
  • d. Uploaded Content
    • Photos taken or uploaded (e.g., schemes or holiday updates)
    • Excel files containing salary slips

2. How We Use the Information

  • Mark attendance and log work hours
  • Track employee movement for field sales management
  • Manage and display shop lists, sale records, and reports
  • Process and display salary slips
  • Securely store photos for identification and reporting

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. However, we may share data with:

  • Company HR/Admins for internal operations
  • Cloud services (e.g., Firebase, AWS) for secure storage
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and cloud security measures (e.g., Firebase Security Rules) to protect your data from unauthorized access.

5. User Rights

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion or correction of your data
  • Withdraw permission for location tracking (note: this may limit app functionality)

6. Location Tracking Notice

This app collects location data even when the app is closed or not in use for the purpose of employee movement tracking and attendance validation. You can manage location permissions from your phone settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active and for internal records for up to 3 months after the last usage unless legally required otherwise.

8. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services like:

  • Google Maps (for location tracking)
  • Firebase (for crashlytics)
  • Lottie Animation (for animations)

Their data handling is governed by their respective privacy policies.
